Here's my site plan for the final project!

Site Name: CrystalDetail

Site Purpose: To inform visitors about professional car detailing services, showcase those services dynamically, and let users contact or book a service easily. Plus, it educates visitors about the benefits of car detailing with interactive features.

Scenarios:

"How do I know which detailing package is best for my car's condition?"

"Can I book a detailing service online?"

"What are the benefits of regular car detailing?"

Color Schema

  • Primary Text Color: #CCE5FF
  • Secondary Color: #A9DFBF
  • Accent Color: #DDD
  • Background Color: #1A1D53

Typography: Bona Nova SC
